LBの Zookeeper Corner: Shinzaki-onii-san

Tell us about the "serval".

They live in regions of Africa called savannahs. Grass grows there, and servals have long limbs in order to walk easily. They have large ears, so they can hear faraway sounds and underground prey. They have jumping strength, and like high places, so servals can easily jump to heights of 1 or 2 metres.

What kind of personality do "servals" have?

They are tremendously inquisitive. They get bored easily when nothing happens, so we have serval jumps (a zoo event) where we give them toys, as well as means of giving them stimuli.

Is there anything you would like people to see?

I think that would be their expressions…. If their personalities differ, they way they think and act differs as well, and this manifests in their expressions. I think that if those are shown with the utmost effort, people will be slowly charmed by not only servals but by the allure of animals.
